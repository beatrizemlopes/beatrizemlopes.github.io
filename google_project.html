The Challenge

How might we give more autonomy to people with reduced mobility when travelling in the city?

With the cobblestone streets and the narrow sidewalks of Lisbon as our starting point, we tried to find how to make exploring less difficult for wheelchair users and other travellers with low mobility.

We chose to work with Google Maps because, besides having over 1 billion users per month and being the most widely used navigation app in the world, Google has been focusing more and more on improving the integration of minorities in its platforms.
