About

Innovative Designer based in Lisbon and passionate about Accessibility and Responsible Design, between the digital and physical worlds.

With a focus on creating inclusive and user-friendly experiences, I have a background in Communication Design and Digital Experience Design.

As a responsible designer, I prioritise ethical and sustainable design practices in my work and strive to create accessible designs that consider the needs of all users.
